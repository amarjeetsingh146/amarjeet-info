Think and Grow Rich

My thoughts · July 2024

This book is less about money and more about belief. Wealth here is a byproduct of obsession, clarity, and persistence.

The mind accepts what it repeatedly hears. Rich or poor is decided long before money appears.

What stayed with me most was the idea that desire must be specific. Vague ambition produces vague results.

— Amarjeet Singh
