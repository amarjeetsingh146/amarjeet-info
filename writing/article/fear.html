Fear

May 4, 2024

Fear is not the enemy. Comfort is. Fear warns you that something matters, while comfort convinces you to stay exactly where you are.

Every meaningful transformation begins with discomfort. Fear appears when you stand at the edge of growth, questioning whether the leap is worth it.

Fear does not stop you — your interpretation of it does.

The bravest people are not fearless. They simply act despite the trembling inside. They understand that fear fades, regret does not.

— Amarjeet Singh
